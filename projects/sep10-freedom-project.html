SEP10 Freedom Project

The purpose of the SEP10 Freedom Project is to spread awarness of how technology can impact a certain field. I decided to pick the topic of mental health because it is something that has become a big problem and I wanted to look into how technology can make mental health better. Throughout the course of a year I researched, learned new tools & programs, and created a website.

There were many challenges I had making this website but if I had to pick the hardest challenge, it was making my website responsive. For a website to be responsive it means that the format of the website adapts to the size of the screen. It took me weeks to finally get the website to be responsive and even now it is something I should continue to work on. However overall I am proud of what I was able to acomplish.
