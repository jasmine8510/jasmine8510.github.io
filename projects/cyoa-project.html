CYOA Project

The prompt for this project was for me and my partners to create a Chose Your Own Adventure (CYOA) story on github. The class entered serveral prompts into a google form and then we picked which ones we wanted to do. The topic I got was jailbreak. The way that is works is that you read what is shown and then based on that you pick a choice, each choice influencing the story and the ending you get.

The way me and my partner did this was by creating multiple files which contained different parts of the story. Each of the choices are in the form of links and when you click on an option it is linked to another file to show you a new part of the story. The work for this project wasn't that bad and it let us have total creative freedom. The hardest part was communication since when the project started I was in quarantine and only was able to communicate through an app instead of in person. One thing I would like to work on in the future is communication skills between me and other peers.
